FAQs

What Is CBD?

CBD is an abbreviation for Cannabidiol, a chemical compound found naturally in the cannabis plant. It is not mind altering like marijuana. CBD will not make you high. Over the past 40 years there have been numerous studies that highlight the potential benefits of CBD. The human Endocannabinoid System (ECS) is the human system that responds to the use of CBD.

Is There THC In Green Roads CBD Products?

Green Roads CBD is extracted from 100% industrial hemp. After extraction, our products are formulated and tested, yielding results of non-detectable THC.

Will CBD Get Me High?

THC is the chemical in marijuana that produces a 'high'. Industrial hemp has more than 0.03% THC. CBD has less than 0.03% THC at most and is filtered out leaving 100% pure, natural CBD. Green Roads CBD products will not get you high.

Is CBD Legal?

CBD is now fully legal at the federal level under the 2018 Farm Bill and is legal in most States.

Can I Overdose On CBD?

No, cannabinoids have no known toxicity. While everyone’s dosage amount is different for their desired results, we suggest starting a CBD regime in small amounts and increasing the dosage, as needed, to reach desired effect.

here is no detectable THC in Green Roads products.
